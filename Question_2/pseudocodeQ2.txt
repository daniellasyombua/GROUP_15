Input
 2 list of objects one for workers and another one for shifts.

Process

1. Make a list of all workers, including their names, their job, which work times they prefer, the maximum number of hours they can work, and how much rest they need between shifts.
2. Make a list of all work periods, including when each one starts and ends, and what types of workers are needed.
3. Arrange the workers based on their job and when they are available.
4. For each work period, choose workers by checking if they have the needed skills, if the time suits them, and if the rules about working hours and rest are followed.
5. Assign workers to each shift making sure there is no gap between one shift ending and the next one starting.
6. Save the final work plan.

Output
List of objects for scheduled shifts
